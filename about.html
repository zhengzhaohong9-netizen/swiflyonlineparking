Business concept

Swiftly is a platform-based e-business for parking

The concept is built around a two-sided marketplace that connects drivers looking for convenient parking with hosts who have unused spaces.

Business problem

Parking in Honolulu is limited, expensive, and difficult to find

Drivers in busy areas often spend time searching for parking, face unpredictable pricing, and experience stress before they even reach their destination.

Business solution

Swiftly unlocks unused parking through online booking

The platform helps hosts monetize private parking while allowing drivers to search, compare, and reserve spaces more easily online.

E-business strategy

Intermediary model with transaction-based value

Swiftly fits an e-business model that creates value by connecting supply and demand in one digital marketplace.

01

Platform strategy

Swiftly operates as a digital platform where users can discover, compare, and reserve parking listings.

02

Two-sided marketplace

The business serves two groups: drivers seeking parking and hosts providing unused parking spaces.

03

Transaction-based service

The platform can eventually support booking fees, payment processing, and reservation management.

Target users

Built for both demand and supply

  • Drivers looking for convenient, reliable parking in Honolulu
  • Property owners or businesses with unused parking spaces
  • Users who value online booking, time savings, and reduced stress

Value proposition

Swiftly saves time for drivers, reduces parking stress, and creates extra income opportunities for hosts through a clean marketplace experience.
