Marketplace flow

How Swiftly works for both sides of the platform

Swiftly is built as a two-sided marketplace connecting drivers who need parking with hosts who have extra space.

For Drivers

Reserve parking with less stress

1

Search by area

Look up neighborhoods like Waikiki, Ala Moana, Downtown, Kakaako, or UH Manoa.

2

Compare options

Review prices, parking details, and availability before making a decision.

3

Reserve and park

Book a space in advance and arrive knowing exactly where to go.

For Hosts

Turn empty stalls into flexible income

1

List your space

Add an unused parking stall from a condo, apartment, house, or business lot.

2

Set availability

Choose when your space is open and whether you want hourly, daily, or monthly bookings.

3

Earn extra income

Accept bookings and monetize unused parking with minimal effort.

Simple process

Three clear steps

Whether someone is booking a spot or listing one, the platform experience stays simple and transparent.

A

Discover

Users browse the platform for parking locations, prices, and space details.

B

Reserve

Drivers choose a space while hosts manage listing availability and booking windows.

C

Use the space

The booking is fulfilled offline when the driver arrives and parks in the reserved stall.
