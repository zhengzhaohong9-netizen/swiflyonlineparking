How hosting works

Start earning in four straightforward steps

Swiftly is designed to make listing a space feel simple, trustworthy, and manageable.

1

List your parking space

Add your location, space type, and access details so drivers know what you offer.

2

Set your availability and price

Choose the times you want to host and whether the space is best for hourly, daily, or monthly use.

3

Accept bookings

Drivers reserve your space through the platform, reducing confusion and improving convenience.

4

Get paid

Earn recurring income from a spot that might otherwise stay empty during busy hours or seasons.
