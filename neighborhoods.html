Neighborhoods

Where Swiftly can grow across Honolulu

This page highlights the core neighborhoods used in the MVP and why each one matters for parking demand.

Waikiki

High tourism demand, beach traffic, hotel overflow, and nightlife make private parking especially valuable.

Ala Moana

Shopping, dining, office visits, and events create steady day-to-day parking demand.

Downtown Honolulu

Professional offices and government buildings create strong weekday booking opportunities.

UH Manoa

Students and staff benefit from predictable recurring monthly or weekly parking access.

Kakaako

Restaurants, art spaces, and waterfront activity support mixed short-term and evening demand.
